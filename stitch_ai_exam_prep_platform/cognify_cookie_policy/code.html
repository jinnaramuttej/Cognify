Transparency is the bedrock of Cognify. This document outlines our architectural approach to data persistence and user identification.

Overview

At Cognify, we leverage technical artifacts commonly known as cookies to optimize our intelligence platform. These small data fragments are stored on your terminal to provide a seamless, high-performance cognitive environment.

These tools are utilized not for surveillance, but for structural integrity. They allow the platform to maintain session continuity, remember personalized interface configurations, and secure user data against unauthorized access.
