Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

01. Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to: Email address, First name and last name, and Biometric usage patterns.

02. Usage Data

We may also collect information how the Service is accessed and used. This Usage Data may include information such as your device's unique identifier, browser type, interaction latencies, and cognitive load indicators during session interactions.
